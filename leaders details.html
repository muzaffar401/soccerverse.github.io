ABOUT

CESAR AZPILICUETA

Cesar Azpilicueta, often known simply as "Azpilicueta" or "Azpi," is a Spanish professional footballer. Born on August 28, 1989, in Pamplona, Spain, he has established himself as a versatile and reliable player in both defense and midfield. Azpilicueta is known for his strong leadership qualities, defensive skills, and consistent performances. He began his professional career at Osasuna in Spain before moving to Marseille in France. In 2012, Azpilicueta signed with Chelsea FC, one of the top clubs in the English Premier League. He quickly became a mainstay in the Chelsea defense due to his work ethic, positional awareness, and ability to play as a right-back or center-back.

Azpilicueta has been a crucial part of Chelsea's successes, winning numerous domestic and international titles with the club, including Premier League titles, FA Cups, and a UEFA Champions League title. On the international stage, Azpilicueta has represented the Spanish national team. His versatility and experience have made him an asset for Spain in major tournaments such as the UEFA European Championship (Euro) and the FIFA World Cup.

Off the pitch, Azpilicueta is known for his sportsmanship, professionalism, and commitment to his team. He is often praised for his leadership on and off the field, earning him the captain's armband at Chelsea. Please note that my information is accurate up to September 2021, and there may have been developments in Cesar Azpilicueta's career since then.
